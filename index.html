Fun with Fourier

There are hundreds of modern technologies, from the compression that is used to store digital images from your phone to the generation of musical notes in a digital piano, that make use of Fourier transformations. But, what is a Fourier transformation? Put simply, the Fourier transform is a series of mathematical equations which allow you to take any signal and discover what underlying frequencies it is made of.
